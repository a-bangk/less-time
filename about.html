About Adam Time

We all live in and share the same time. The clock is always ticking, Adam Time was made with the intent to seperate us from the constant count.

The 24-hour day is divided into 16, 90 minute blocks. Each block is then divided into 初(start) , 中(middle) and 終(end).

There are no time zones in Adam-Time, as we travel the world we develop new relationships to the symbols. 🦋 may go from being the middle of the night to when we wake up. The 🕰️ will take you to Adam-time.
