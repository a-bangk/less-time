About Adam-Time

We all live in and share the same time. The clock is always ticking, Adam-Time was made with the intent to seperate us from the constant count.

The 24-hour day is divided into 16, 90 minute blocks. Each block is then divided into 一, 二 and 三.

There are no time zones in Adam-Time, as we travel the world we develop new relationships to the symbols. 🦋 may go from being the middle of the night to when we wake up. The 🕰️ will show you the current Adam-Time.
